The Clincher - Chevelle

02/27/2023

BPM (Beats Per Minute) = 89  

Key: C# Major

Time: 3:44

Tuning: (A# - F - A# - D# - G - C)


NOTE: Equipment, Guitars,  Gear, etc. …. is not what is used in the original recording. Each player has individual preferences, however it its possible to recreate the general tone using almost any combination of your choice. What follows is my individual preferred method of recreating a similar tone faithful to the original recording, while retaining my individual tonality. 


  • Orange Amplification (Terror Stamp and Crush 20)
  • Jackson JS Dinky Series with High Output Humbuckers
  • Ernie Ball Beefy Slinky Strings (11-54)
  • Ibanez TS9 TubeScreamer
  • BOSS SD-1 SuperOverdrive
  • Reverb
  • Chorus
  • Compression
  • Noise Gate
  • Dunlop Crybaby


The song features a modern “scooped” midrange frequency tone, meaning the midrange is lowered, while the bass and treble are increased. This sound is fairly easy to produce on most amplifiers, however you will need a high-gain amplifier with sufficient equalizerization. The sound is further enhanced by using a compression pedal to decrease the level of sustain, increase the attack, and keep your level’s at 12 Noon. This “tightens” up the sound, leaving less of a sound tail after each note is played in rapid succession. Placing a Ibanez TubeScreamer, all set to 12 Noon,  in front of your amplifiers distortion is really the secret to attaining an even more articulate sound, while not mudding up the signal clarity or intonation.
